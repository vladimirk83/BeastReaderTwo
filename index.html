Lottery App Manual (English)

Date Selection: The date input lets you pick one or multiple betting dates. Today is automatically assigned.

Tracks: Expand the “USA” or “Santo Domingo” sections. Mark the tracks you want. Some tracks will disable after cutoff time. If you pick “Venezuela,” combine it with a USA track to do Venezuelan plays. For “NY Horses,” you can enter 1–4 digits and it will appear as “NY Horses.”

Plays Table: Each row has a Bet Number, which determines the game mode (Pulito, Pick 3, Win 4, Single Action, NY Horses, etc.). Straight/Box/Combo amounts can be added. The total for that row is displayed. The overall total is multiplied by your selected dates and tracks.

Single Action: If you pick a normal USA track (not Venezuela, not NY Horses) and enter exactly 1 digit in Bet Number.

NY Horses: If track is “New York Horses,” 1–4 digits becomes that mode.

Buttons: Add Play, Wizard, Remove Last, Reset, Generate Ticket.

Quick Entry Window (Wizard): You can lock amounts (Straight, Box, Combo), generate Quick Picks, Round Down, or Permute digits. Then “Add Main” or “Generate Ticket.”

Ticket Preview: Shows your plays, total, and a QR code. You can confirm & print/share. A unique Ticket Number is assigned once confirmed.

Manual Completo de la App de Lotería (Español)

Selección de Fechas: Puede elegir una o varias fechas. “Hoy” se asigna por defecto.

Tracks: Expanda “USA” o “Santo Domingo.” Marque los que desee. “Venezuela” requiere un track de USA. “NY Horses” permite 1–4 dígitos.

Tabla de Jugadas: Cada fila con Bet Number define el modo (Pulito, Win4, Pick3, etc.). Los montos Straight/Box/Combo se suman en la fila. Luego se multiplica por fechas y tracks.

Single Action: 1 dígito en track USA (no Venezuela, no Horses). “NY Horses” => 1–4 dígitos.

Botones: Add Play, Wizard, Remove Last, Reset, Generate Ticket.

Ventana Wizard: Permite “lock” Straight/Box/Combo, Quick Picks, Round Down, Permute. Después “Add Main” o “Generate Ticket.”

Vista Previa del Ticket: Muestra jugadas, total, QR. Al confirmar, asigna número único y se descarga imagen. Puede compartirla.

Manyèl Aplikasyon Lòtri (Kreyòl)

Chwazi Dat: Ou ka pran youn oswa plizyè dat, “jodia” default.

Tracks: “USA” / “Santo Domingo.” “Venezuela” bezwen track USA. “NY Horses” se 1–4 chif.

Tab Pari: Chak ranje gen Bet Number => Pulito, Win4, Pick3, etc. Mete montan Straight/Box/Combo. Total la miltipliye pa dat ak tracks.

Single Action: 1 chif si se track USA (pa Venezuela, pa Horses). “NY Horses” => 1–4 chif.

Bouton: Add Play, Wizard, Remove Last, Reset, Generate Ticket.

Fenèt Wizard: “lock” Straight/Box/Combo, Quick Picks, Round Down, Permute. Apre sa “Add Main” oswa “Generate Ticket.”

Preview Ticket: Montre parye yo, total la, ak kòd QR. Konfime => bay tikè a yon nimewo inik, telechaje imaj, ka pataje li.
